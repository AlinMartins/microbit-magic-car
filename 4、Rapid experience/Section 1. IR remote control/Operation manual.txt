
Key A of the infrared remote control is pressed to control the onboard RGB lamp
Button B of infrared remote control is pressed to control steering gear rotation (steering gear Angle is adjusted according to the actual assembly situation)
Infrared remote control button C is pressed to control the headlights
The button up of the infrared remote control is pressed to move the car forward
The button down of the infrared remote control is pressed and the car backs up
The right button of the infrared remote control is pressed and the car turns right
The left button of the infrared remote control is pressed and the car turns left
Infrared remote control button D is pressed, the car left turn on the spot
Infrared remote control button 0 is pressed, the car on the right
Infrared remote control button OK is pressed, car stop
Infrared remote control button + is pressed, car buzzer sound
